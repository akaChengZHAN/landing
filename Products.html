Design Concept---Christmas Rabbit Series

The first time we launched our IP products, we wanted to convey our product concept of love
and happiness, so we designed the Christmas rabbit series by combining Chinese and Western elements.
In Chinese culture, the rabbit is usually regarded as a symbol of beauty, gentleness, kindness and purity.
Its image often appears in poems, novels, plays and other literary works. In addition, when facing difficulties,
the image of the rabbit encourages people to be positive and courageous in overcoming challenges, which
signifies transcending oneself, moving towards success and achieving what one wishes for. This is in line
with our Pure Love product philosophy.As one of the biggest religious festivals in the world, Christmas has
become an important part of secular culture. Christmas is not just the most festive and joyous time of the
year, theThe festival represents new beginnings: every year Christmas is a time of new beginnings, when people
look back on the past year, set New Year's goals and look to the future with hope. It also signifies the launch
of a company that is full of hope and can bring joy.
